# Toggle — grouped accordion

Group related disclosures with `data-automagica11y-group` so opening one item closes any other expanded panels. This mirrors classic accordion behavior without additional JavaScript.

<Playground id="example-toggle-accordion" heading="Grouped accordion" description="Only one panel stays open at a time.">
  <div slot="demo" class="docs-example-accordion">
    <button
      data-automagica11y-toggle="#example-toggle-accordion-a"
      data-automagica11y-group="faq"
      data-automagica11y-trigger-class-open="docs-example-accordion__trigger--open"
      class="docs-example-accordion__trigger"
    >
      Shipping
    </button>
    <section id="example-toggle-accordion-a" hidden class="docs-example-accordion__panel">
      <p>Standard orders ship within 3–5 business days. Express options are available at checkout.</p>
    </section>

    <button
      data-automagica11y-toggle="#example-toggle-accordion-b"
      data-automagica11y-group="faq"
      data-automagica11y-trigger-class-open="docs-example-accordion__trigger--open"
      class="docs-example-accordion__trigger"
    >
      Returns
    </button>
    <section id="example-toggle-accordion-b" hidden class="docs-example-accordion__panel">
      <p>Start a return within 30 days for a prepaid label. Exchanges are processed immediately.</p>
    </section>

    <button
      data-automagica11y-toggle="#example-toggle-accordion-c"

      <p>Specialists are online 8 a.m.–8 p.m. ET Monday through Friday and 10 a.m.–4 p.m. on weekends.</p>
    </section>
  </div>
  <div slot="code">
{`<button

  </div>

When one trigger opens, any other toggle in the same group automatically closes. This keeps focus on the current section while maintaining accessible disclosure semantics.
